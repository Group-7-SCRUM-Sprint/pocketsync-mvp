# PocketSync

## Overview

PocketSync is a consolidated personal finance management platform designed to help Nigerians manage multiple bank and fintech accounts from a single interface.

Many users maintain accounts across providers such as GTBank, Access Bank, Kuda, Opay, Moniepoint, and PalmPay. Switching between multiple applications to check balances, review spending, and make financial decisions creates friction and reduces visibility into overall financial health.

PocketSync addresses this challenge by providing a unified experience where users can view their finances in one place, monitor spending habits, and make smarter money management decisions.

---

## Problem Statement

Managing finances across multiple banking and fintech platforms is fragmented and time-consuming. Users often lack a complete picture of their financial position, making budgeting, spending control, and account selection difficult.

PocketSync aims to simplify financial management by delivering a centralized and trustworthy platform for account aggregation and financial insights.

## Sprint Goal

Develop a functional MVP within a 5-day Scrum sprint that focuses on delivering one core user value effectively.

### Selected MVP Feature

**Account Aggregation Dashboard**

Users can:

* Connect multiple bank and fintech accounts
* View combined account balances
* Access a unified financial overview
* Monitor spending activity across accounts
* Gain visibility into their overall financial health
